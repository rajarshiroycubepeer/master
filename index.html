    • ABOUT US

    Shriram Enterprises is founded in Bhilai, Chhattisgarh (India), the land known for its highest industrial density. The organization operates across multiple industries including Steel, Cement, Power, Construction and Healthcare. We are one among the fastest growing organisation in Chhattisgarh with an unimaginable year on year growth rate of 48%, which become possible for us to achieve only because of our highly customer centric approach and dedicated work force for every industry

    Shriram Enterprises has been established on three basis pillars that is Read, Research and Remit. Our approach of problem solving is very systematic. We believe in understanding a problem (Read), than we do a proper research on how to solve it (Research) and at the last we propose the solutions to the concern organization (Remit).

    Our team is highly qualified and experienced enough to go deep into the problem and come up with a solution. It's our understanding that the time has always been a limited resource for anyone in this world, so for us too. Timely delivery of solutions to our clients is always on our priority.

    Our quality products, path-breaking delivery schedule, adaptive approach and a fair business practice are all to imprint a memorable experience to our clients. Our endeavour is to deliver simple yet robust solutions to all the associated clients and help them to reduce complexity and enable flexible, agile and safe service delivery.
